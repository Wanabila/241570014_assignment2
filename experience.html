One of the experiences I remember the most is after completing my STPM. At that time, my friends and I made plans to watch the sunrise at a beach in Besut. We had been planning this for a long time, but because some of us could not join, we had to postpone it. In the end, we managed to fulfill that plan. On the day of the event, we left the house at 5 AM. The journey took more than an hour from our house. We arrived at the beach at 6:45 AM. When we arrived at the beach, we witnessed a very beautiful beach and sunrise. Although the journey was quite far, we enjoyed it very much. By doing this activity, we were able to gather some beautiful memories.
